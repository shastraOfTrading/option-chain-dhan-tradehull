Strongest Support i
What is Support? The strike with the highest Put OI below ATM. Put writers (sellers) here earn premium only if NIFTY stays ABOVE this level — so they defend it. The more put OI, the stronger the floor.
📌 If 23500 has 12L PE OI and someone adds 2.4L today, they are betting big that NIFTY won't fall below 23500.

Option LTP Calculator i
How this works Formula: Expected LTP = Current LTP + (NIFTY Move × Delta).

Delta = how much the option price changes per 1 point move in NIFTY. This is a linear approximation — actual gain may differ due to Gamma and IV changes.
📌 Example: LTP=145, Delta=0.40, NIFTY +100 pts → Expected LTP = 145 + (100 × 0.40) = ₹185
